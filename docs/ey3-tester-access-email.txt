WHAT KAIMI DOES
Kaimi is your automated federal BD analyst. It hunts SAM.gov for opportunities,
scores each one for fit against YOUR business, and helps you draft proposals —
with you in control and approving every step. The more it learns about your
company, the sharper it gets.

  2) Complete the quick setup wizard:

     • Company profile — your company name, NAICS codes, core competencies, and

     • SAM.gov API key — paste your own key so Kaimi searches on your quota. If

     • Context documents — upload your capability statement, a past proposal, or
       a CPARS. Kaimi reads these to understand your business. PDF, Word (.docx),
       or text all work. This step is optional but makes everything better.

  3) Done. Kaimi builds your "capability map" — a synthesized picture of what your
     company does, grounded in your profile and documents — and begins scoring
     opportunities for you.

WHAT TO EXPLORE

  • Open any opportunity to see full details, deadlines, and attachments.
